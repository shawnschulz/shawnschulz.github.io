Hello and welcome to my website! I'm Shawn, an aspiring software engineer and computational biologist.

My goal is to help develop computational infrastructure and machine learning models to discover treatments and outline disease pathologies for incurable or high impact dieseases like cancer, heart disease and infectious diseases. I am also interested in using machine learning to model a variety of problems, as well as to automate tasks in biotechnology.

I am currently working on a CNC controlled micropipette tool and writing a transformer architecture based model for cancer pathway detection and protein solving.

In my free time I like biking, playing guitar and developing video games.
